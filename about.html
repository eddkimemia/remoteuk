About RemoteMedicalJobs

We help healthcare professionals worldwide find remote and hybrid opportunities — and get career-ready with practical telehealth training.

Our platform combines a curated job board with an optional Career Accelerator. After you purchase the course, access is delivered to your email with a private portal link. Training covers consultation skills, scheduling workflows, privacy essentials (HIPAA / GDPR-style practices), CVs, and interviews for a global market.
