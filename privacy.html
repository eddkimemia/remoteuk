Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

At RemoteMedicalJobs.co.uk, we are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of our clinical applicants, healthcare professionals, and website users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, store, and secure your personal and clinical data in complete compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), Data Protection Act 2018, and the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) principles for handling sensitive patient and medical workforce information.

1. Information We Collect

To evaluate your qualification for telehealth and clinical remote roles, we collect the following information:

  • Candidate Profile Details: First Name, Last Name, Email address, WhatsApp number, Country of Residence, Years of Clinical Experience, and Speciality/Profession.
  • Clinical Documents: CVs, resumes, licenses, and certifications uploaded during the eligibility assessment.
  • Payment Information: Financial transactions are processed securely via Paystack. We do not store or process your credit card details directly on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your clinical data solely for the following legitimate business purposes:

  • Verifying eligibility for remote clinic placements and healthcare partner interviews.
  • Facilitating secure enrollment in the Telehealth Compliance & Scheduling Certification.
  • Updating you on relevant job application outcomes and interview schedules.
  • Improving our website funnel and ensuring data security.

3. Data Protection (GDPR & HIPAA Compliance)

We implement top-tier technical and organizational security measures to protect your sensitive clinical records:

  • Encryption: All personal data, file uploads, and communications are encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS and at rest using AES-256 encryption.
  • Access Control: Access to applicant records is strictly restricted to vetted compliance officers.
  • Retention: We retain clinical records for only as long as necessary to fulfill candidate-matching services or comply with clinical audit regulations.

4. Your Rights

Under the UK GDPR, you hold the following rights regarding your data:

  • The right to access the data we hold about you.
  • The right to request immediate correction of inaccurate data.
  • The right to request erasure of your clinical CV or candidate profile ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • The right to withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

5. Contact Us

For any data protection inquiries, or to request erasure of your candidate record, please contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at hello@remotemedicaljobs.co.uk.
