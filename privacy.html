Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 · Applies worldwide

RemoteMedicalJobs (“we”, “us”) operates a global remote healthcare job board and Career Accelerator certification product. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. Information we collect

  • Account / application details: name, email, phone (optional), country, profession, experience, job interest, free-text goals.
  • Enrollment & payment metadata: product purchased, amount, Paystack reference, payment status. We do not store full card numbers — payments are processed by Paystack.
  • Contact messages: content you send via the contact form.
  • Technical data: basic server logs (IP, user agent) for security and debugging.

CV file uploads are not stored on our servers at this time. If you email a CV to support, it is processed only to respond to your request.

2. How we use information

  • Match candidates with remote role interest and employer outreach (where applicable).
  • Process course purchases and email course access links / portal tokens.
  • Respond to support requests and improve the product.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse (rate limits, payment verification).

3. Legal bases & global privacy

Depending on your location, we rely on contract performance (providing the course/job tools you request), legitimate interests (securing the service), and/or consent where required. We design training content to respect common privacy regimes (including GDPR-style and HIPAA-oriented workplace practices) but we are a career platform — not your employer’s covered entity or data controller for clinical patient records.

4. Sharing

  • Paystack — payment processing.
  • Email/SMTP provider — transactional course and contact emails.
  • Hosting providers — infrastructure.
  • We do not sell personal data.

5. Retention & security

Enrollment and application records are retained as long as needed for delivery, support, accounting, and legitimate business records, then deleted or anonymised on request where feasible. We use HTTPS in production, access controls on servers, and least-privilege practices. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

6. Your rights

Subject to local law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or export of your data, or object to certain processing. Contact hello@remotemedicaljobs.com with the email you used on the site.

7. International transfers

We serve a worldwide audience. Your data may be processed in countries other than your own by us and our subprocessors, with safeguards appropriate to the service.
